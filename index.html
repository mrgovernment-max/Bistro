Our Story

Welcome to Betty's Bistro

Betty's Bistro is a family-run restaurant, nestled on the busy Oxford Road in Reading west. Betty offers a selection of her most popular dishes, showcasing her twist on traditional Ghanaian flavors introduced to her by her mother and grandmother.

We serve slow-cooked food packed with irresistible flavors that give a real taste of home-cooked Ghanaian food. The exuberant Ghanaian hospitality, expressed heartily in the traditional greeting "Akwaaba" (meaning "welcome"), will warm you.

You will feel at home at Betty's Bistro while discovering its unparalleled traditions. Whatever your mood, one of our meals will match it. Our attention to detail ensures that every action we take results in Total Customer Satisfaction.
